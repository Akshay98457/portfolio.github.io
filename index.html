Overview of the Course

The "Design and Analysis of Algorithms" course dives into the principles of algorithm design and data structures. From foundational concepts like recursion and complexity to advanced structures such as AVL Trees and Red-Black Trees, it covers practical and theoretical aspects essential for solving real-world problems.

Applications in the Real World

Algorithms are not just theoretical constructs; they have real-world applications in diverse fields, such as:

  • Smart Cities: Optimizing transportation systems with Dijkstra's algorithm.
  • Data Management: Enhancing database performance with sorting techniques.
  • Problem Solving: Tackling logistics challenges using dynamic programming.
